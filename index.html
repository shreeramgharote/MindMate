MindMate

AI-Powered Mental Wellness Assistant for Students

About MindMate

MindMate is an AI-powered chatbot developed to support students dealing with stress, anxiety, motivation issues, academic pressure, and mental wellness challenges.

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

This project contributes to Sustainable Development Goal 3 by promoting mental health awareness and providing accessible support for students.

Key Features

  • Stress Management Support
  • Anxiety Assistance
  • Motivational Guidance
  • Study Tips
  • Mental Health Resources
  • 24/7 AI Support

Future Scope

  • Voice-Based Interaction
  • Multilingual Support
  • Mobile Application
  • Sentiment Analysis
  • Counselor Integration
